Journal of International Special Needs Education

The journal is exclusively an e-journal.

The purpose of the Journal of International Special Needs Eduction is to provide DISES members and members of the international scientific community with information that reflects the field of special education around the world. It is a multi-disciplinary journal for reporting original and theoretical contributions concerning disability and the delivery of special education programs and services to individuals with disabilities throughout the world.
