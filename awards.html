About DISES Awards

The DISES Awards give us a unique opportunity to celebrate the accomplishments of our colleagues. Through these awards, we recognize the special educators who have advanced the field and the quality of special education service as well as those members whose contributions have moved the DISES forward in significant ways. Information about the DISES 2022 awards and how to nominate deserving colleagues for the 2022 awards will be announced in the fall of 2021.

DISES 2021 Award

DISES presented special awards to Drs. Mansur Choudry and Margarita Perez Cañadas for making a difference during the pandemic. The awards were given during the CEC L.I.V.E. Convention at the  Social on Friday, March 12, 6:30 pm (ET) following the DISES Business/Membership meeting, 6:00-6:30pm (ET). The awards were based on their contributions during the pandemic.
