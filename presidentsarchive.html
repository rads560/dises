FREE CEC Membership
CEC has made an incredibly generous decision to open up CEC membership to those who may not be members, in case there are resources that CEC has that could be helpful. Please share this information with supervising faculty, cooperating teachers, students, teachers, and others. This is a sincere offer from CEC to help share the brain power at CEC during this unprecedented time.
CEC is opening its doors to the special education community in need of resources. Nonmembers of CEC can receive a free basic membership from now through May 31st by using the promotional code "CECED60". This will give you access to all of CEC's journal articles, member discounts on events, and access to CEC's online membership community where you can ask questions and receive support from special educators, administrators and support personnel. Join now! Early bird registration is now open for the DISES 2020 Dubai conference. You can register using the link here.

• UNESCO provides a list of educational applications and platforms to help parents, teachers, schools and school systems facilitate student learning and provide social caring and interaction during periods of school closure.
