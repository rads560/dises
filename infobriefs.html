Perspectives on Global Inclusive Education: Policies and Effective Practices

This Information Brief is intended to acquaint our international community with effective global inclusive education practices, policies, and frameworks for engaging students with disabilities, while addressing common challenges and resource issues experienced by students and their educators. In this brief, we highlight specific strategies for promoting inclusive education and also offer additional resources that maybe helpful to our global community.
